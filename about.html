Our Story

Executive Summary

Ads Bego is a private investment partnership that leverages disciplined portfolio management across stocks, commodities, and cryptocurrencies to deliver risk-adjusted returns. We combine active trading strategies with long-term asset appreciation to grow partner capital.

  • Diversified Exposure: 40/30/30 allocation across three high-growth asset classes.
  • Structured Plans: Tailored options for short-term (12-month) and long-term (3-year) investors.
  • Transparent Operations: Quarterly performance reporting and full risk disclosure.

Investment Portfolio Structure

Our carefully calibrated portfolio allocation maximizes returns while managing risk through diversification:

Primary Allocation - Shares/Equities:

70% of our portfolio is invested in carefully selected public companies across growing sectors including technology, healthcare, and renewable energy.

Securities Trading - Diversified Approach:

20% of our capital is actively managed across three key markets:

  • Stock Trading (40%): Short to medium-term positions in high-potential equities
  • Binance Cryptocurrency (30%): Strategic positions in major cryptocurrencies and select altcoins
  • Forex Trading (30%): Currency pair trading with emphasis on major global currencies

Sustainable Investments - Agro-Trade:

10% allocated to Perus Agro-Trade, supporting sustainable agriculture and commodity trading with strong social impact.
